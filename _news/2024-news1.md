  MultiBanAbs: A Comprehensive Multi-Domain Bangla Abstractive Text Summarization Dataset
date: 2025-11-05 10:00:00 -0600
categories: [Research, TCCE]

This paper introduces **MultiBanAbs**, the largest multi-domain Bangla abstractive summarization dataset, comprising 54,620 articles and summaries from diverse sources.  
The dataset is evaluated with **BanglaT5, mT5-small, and LSTM models**, demonstrating the effectiveness of transformer-based approaches for Bangla text summarization.
